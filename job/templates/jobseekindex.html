About JobSeeker Project

The JobSeeker platform is designed to connect job seekers with potential employers. It provides a seamless experience for users to create and manage profiles, search for job listings, and apply to relevant opportunities. This dashboard offers a personalized space where you can explore job openings that match your profile, receive recommendations, and track your applications. Built with an intuitive interface and efficient backend technology, JobSeeker aims to simplify the job-hunting process, making it easy and effective.
